This is a demonstrator for the symbolic math library for javascript.

The library is under development. Not all math operations are supported, and there are undoubtably bugs.

Enter your equation below. Hit the "simplify" button and see the result.

Equations need to be formatted as follows:

  • Addition: A+B
  • Subtraction: A-B
  • Multiplication: A&dot;B
  • Division: A/B
  • Exponent: A<sup>B</sup>
  • Partial Differential: &part;x(A)
